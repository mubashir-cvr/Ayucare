Over View

Pain is a discomfiting sensation that tells something is happening wrong in certain body parts. The unpleasant feeling that is experienced may be a prick, tingle, sting, burn or ache. The pain may affect different parts of the body such as the neck, back, shoulder, knee or heel. The part that is affected may vary but all come under the same strategies of treatment. Ayurcare Rasyana Clinics operates to cure the suffering caused by pain. For completely curing the pain, it is vital to understand the root cause of the pain. Once the root cause is determined the necessary treatment methods should be delivered to remove it from the root and prevent it from becoming chronic pain. The pain can be relieved by appropriate dieting, the right lifestyle and using natural herbs(Ayurcare uses Arka as medicine which is extracted by distilling natural herbs).

Symptoms

Back pain can be simple muscle aching or a shooting, burning sensation. Proper medical assistance is required if the back pain persists over a week, is severe and no improvement on resting and pain, weakness and numbness on one or both legs. Back pain may accompany by excess weight loss and requires immediate medical care.

Causes

People suffer back pain due to sprain and strain and conditions that lead to back pain are Muscular or LIgament strain (Heavy Lifting and sudden awkward movement cause strain in back muscles and ligaments), Bulging or ruptured disk(Bulging or Ruptured soft materials will press on the nerves causing difficulty), Arthritis (Osteoarthritis effect on the lower back and arthritis on the spine is termed as Spinal Stenosis) and osteoporosis (Painful fractures on the spine’s vertebrae due to porous and brittle bones).
